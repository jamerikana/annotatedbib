About Amilia

Amilia has worked in industry for over 13 years in bid and proposals for government contracts. Her depth of knowledge in the proposal development process makes her a valuable asset to proposal teams having years of experience as a technical writer, desktop publisher, proposal coordinator, and proposal manager.

Currently, Amilia is a Doctoral Student in Rhetoric and Writing at Virginia Tech. She is developing new ideas and methodologies while studying that convergence of her professional knowledge and academic scholarship. Amilia maintains industry relationships to not lose touch with what is current in industry and shares this information with her students.

Amilia holds a M.A. in Technical Communication from Texas Tech, a B.A. in English with a minor in business from Virginia Tech, a Graduate Certificate in Teaching Technical Communication and a Graduate Certificate in Grants and Proposals from Texas Tech. Her research interests are in African American Rhetoric, Intercultural Communication, Cross-Cultural Communication, and Technical Communication.
